From my heart

In the halls of our shared past, From our Year 1 days that flew so fast,
Through laughter, tears, and childish fights, We've grown together, reaching greater heights.

Though words unsaid may fill the air, Know my love for you is always there,
In every smile, every shared glance, In moments of joy and in the dance.

We've weathered storms, our bond so strong, Through arguments, righting every wrong,
For in the end, it's clear to see, Our friendship is a cherished decree.

So on this day, your special day, I send my love in every way,
For you, my friend, mean more than words can say, Happy Birthday, in every possible way.
